ABOUT US

Increase your expertise in business, technology and personal development

Train & Place combines art and technology by training individuals possessing a creative mind and advancing them into the modern competitive business environment. Set up keeping in mind the ideals of a productive and innovative organization, our mission is to train curious candidates to make a global impact with their proficiency and knowledge gained from us. Our team of professionals ensure that the candidates work through a systematic plan along with an artistic inclination to solve problems.

Train & Place holds different IT domains under its umbrella and creates a fascinating learning experience by providing with the career development tools, support and job training. From one-to-one meeting with our experts, to transparent and regular feedbacks with a long-term career plan, we enhance the creative concepts of the individuals that appeal to the eye of the audience and culminate their journey from college to the corporate world.

We are on a mission to equip candidates with relevant skills and practical exposure by advancing them into the professional world and exploring its realms through our job offers. We help connect people to technology by changing the way the world works by encouraging our candidates to transform their dreams into reality by submitting in hard work and passion.
